Initialize a ccask key-value object.

The parameters key and value are copied. It is the callers responsibility to free the original key/value objects. The internal key/value objects will be free'd when ccask_kv_delete is called.

Parameters
kvthe uninitialized ccask_kv pointer to initialize

valuea pointer to the first byte of the value.
